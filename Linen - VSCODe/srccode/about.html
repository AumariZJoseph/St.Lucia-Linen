Who Are We?

Saint Lucia’s Premier Linen Service Provider

For over three decades, we’ve delivered quality textile rental and commercial laundry services to Saint Lucia’s hospitality and healthcare sectors. As a 100% locally owned enterprise established in 1990, we combine island expertise with global standards.

Our seasoned team brings hands-on experience in all aspects of linen rental and commercial laundry services. Through substantial investments in high-production machinery from world-leading manufacturers, we maintain unmatched operational efficiency while upholding our environmental commitments.
